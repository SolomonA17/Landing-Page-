Key Features

Real-Time Weather Updates

Stay ahead of the weather with real-time updates that provide the most current information about temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and more. Our data is sourced from reliable meteorological providers to ensure accuracy. Whether you're planning your day or looking ahead, Forecastify keeps you informed with up-to-the-minute weather conditions tailored to your location.

Week Long Forecast

Plan your week with confidence using our comprehensive seven-day forecast. Get detailed daily predictions, including temperature highs and lows, chance of rain, and wind conditions. This feature is perfect for scheduling outdoor activities, travel plans, and more. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to view and interpret the weather trends for the entire week at a glance.

Detailed Forecasting

Dive deeper into the weather details with our detailed forecasting feature. Access in-depth data such as hourly temperature changes, humidity levels, UV index, sunrise and sunset times, and more. This feature is designed for users who need precise weather information, whether you're a farmer planning your crops, a pilot preparing for a flight, or just someone who loves to stay informed. Our detailed forecasts help you make well-informed decisions based on accurate and comprehensive weather data.

About Forecastify

Forecastify was born from the frustration of finding accurate, personalized weather information in Hawassa, Ethiopia. This project serves as a portfolio piece for Holberton School.

As the lead developer, I, Solomon Aberra, took charge of designing and implementing the core features of Forecastify. My responsibilities included integrating real-time weather updates, developing the week-long forecast feature, and ensuring detailed forecasting accuracy. My goal was to create an application that not only provides accurate weather information but also presents it in an intuitive and user-friendly manner.
